About Our School

Nurturing Minds, Building Character

Oakk Wood School is a premier English medium, co-educational institution located in the heart of Bajpur, Uttarakhand. We believe every child possesses unique potential waiting to be discovered and developed.

Our experienced faculty, modern infrastructure, and holistic approach to education ensure that students receive the best preparation for their future — academically, socially, and emotionally.
